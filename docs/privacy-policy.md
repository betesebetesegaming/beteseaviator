## 3. What personal data we collect

**a) Information you give us when you register or use the service**
- Full name
- Mobile phone number (used to sign in and to receive one-time codes)
- Password (stored only in a hashed/encrypted form — we never store it in plain text and
  cannot see it)
- Your confirmation that you are 18 or older and eligible to play in The Gambia
- Any information you give us when you contact Customer Care

**b) Account and gameplay information we generate**
- Your Player ID and account status
- Wallet balance and full transaction history (deposits, withdrawals, bets, wins, bonuses)
- Game activity (games played, stakes, results, session times)
- Referral relationships and, where applicable, the agent or marketer linked to your account

**c) Payment information**
- Deposits and withdrawals are processed by our payment partner **ModemPay**. Payment details
  you enter are handled by the payment provider; we receive transaction records (amount,
  status, reference) but not your full payment credentials.
- For over-the-counter (cash desk) transactions handled by an agent, we record the amount,
  the agent involved, and a withdrawal code.

**d) Verification data**
- One-time passcodes (OTPs) sent by SMS to your registered phone number to verify your
  identity and to authorise cash transactions.

**e) Technical and device data**
- IP address, device and browser type, and similar technical identifiers
- Cookies and local storage used to keep you signed in and to operate the site (see Section 9)

## 4. How we use your data

We use your personal data to:
- Create and manage your account and verify your identity and age
- Let you deposit, place bets, play games, and withdraw winnings
- Send one-time codes and authorise sensitive actions (such as cash deposits and withdrawals)
- Keep records of transactions and prevent fraud, money laundering, and abuse
- Provide Customer Care and respond to your requests
- Operate our agent/marketer and referral programmes and calculate commissions
- Improve the service and, where enabled, offer you personalised bonuses and promotions
  (see Section 5)
- Meet our legal, regulatory, tax, and responsible-gambling obligations

## 5. Automated processing and personalised offers ("Smart Bonus")

Where enabled, we use an automated system that analyses your account activity (such as how
often and how much you play and deposit) to decide which bonuses, rewards, or promotions to
offer you. This is used only to tailor offers and retention rewards — it does **not** decide
whether you can withdraw your money or close your account. If you would like a human to review
an offer decision, contact Customer Care.

## 6. When we share your data

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only as needed to run the service:

- **Payment provider (ModemPay):** to process deposits and withdrawals.
- **Game provider / aggregator (QTech Games and its game studios):** to launch games and record
  bets, wins, and game sessions.
- **SMS / OTP provider:** to deliver one-time codes to your phone.
- **Hosting and infrastructure providers (Vercel; Google Firebase / Google Cloud):** who host
  the app and store the database and account records on our behalf.
- **Agents and marketers:** an agent or marketer linked to your account can see limited account
  information (such as your Player ID, name, phone, balance, and cash transactions they handle)
  in order to serve you at the shop.
- **Authorities and regulators:** where required by law, licence conditions, fraud prevention,
  or anti-money-laundering rules.
- **Professional advisers or in a business transfer:** e.g. auditors, or if the business is
  reorganised or sold.

## 7. International transfers

Some of our service providers (for example hosting and database services) may store or process
data on servers **outside The Gambia**. Where this happens, we take reasonable steps to ensure
your data remains protected to a comparable standard.

## 8. How long we keep your data

We keep your account and transaction data for as long as your account is active and for as long
afterwards as we are required to by law, tax, licensing, and anti-money-laundering rules, and to
resolve disputes and prevent fraud. When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymise it.

## 9. Cookies and local storage

We use cookies and browser local storage to keep you signed in, remember your session, operate
core features, and keep the service secure. Essential cookies are required for the site to work.
You can control cookies in your browser settings, but disabling essential cookies may stop you
from signing in or playing.

## 10. How we protect your data

- Passwords are stored hashed — never in plain text.
- Sensitive actions (cash deposits and withdrawals) require a one-time code sent to your phone.
- Access to customer data is restricted by role (customer, agent, admin).
- Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).

No system is 100% secure. Keep your password and one-time codes private — BETESE staff and
agents will **never** ask you for your password, and you should only share a one-time code to
authorise a transaction you have requested.

- Withdraw consent to marketing at any time
